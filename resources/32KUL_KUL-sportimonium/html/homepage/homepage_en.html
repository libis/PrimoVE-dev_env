Library and Documentation Centre

Are you researching the Flemish and Belgian sporting past? Then be sure to explore our library collection. It includes more than 14,000 books, brochures, and journals on (pre-)modern Belgian sports history.

Researchers can also consult our extensive collection of archives and documentary materials. Sportimonium preserves more than 50 archival collections of athletes, sports clubs, and sports federations. In addition, we manage several large documentary collections on Belgian sports history.

For more information, visit the Sportimonium website
