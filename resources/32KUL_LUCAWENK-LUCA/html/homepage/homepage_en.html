What is Limo?

LIMO is a discovery tool for searching across a variety of print and electronic resources of LUCA School of Arts as well as other LIBISnet libraries. It facilitates discovery of books, e-books, full-text journal articles, theses, research publications, open access resources and more, all in one single search.

Campus
You can limit your search results to local items by selecting your campus: physical books, journals, articles, DVD's, games, etc.

Why log in?

  • Access e-sources that are only available to LUCA staff and students
  • Manage your loans and requests
  • Request items on loan
  • View your loan history

  • Save searches and create alerts for newly published content
