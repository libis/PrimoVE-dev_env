What can you find in Limo?

Limo allows you to search the physical and digital collections of Thomas More and other institutions within Associatie KU Leuven.
You can:

  • Find the location and call number of (print) books, journals and other materials held by libraries and learning centres of Thomas More. Limit your search to your own campus to get accurate results.

  • Get access to numerous e-books and e-journals as part of scholarly databases and Open Access collections to which Thomas More has a subscription.
