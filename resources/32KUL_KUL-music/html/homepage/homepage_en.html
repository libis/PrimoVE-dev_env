Welcome to the Music Catalog of Limo
You will find here scores, recordings and printed and electronic publications related to music belonging to the music collections of KU Leuven Libraries (Artes and MATRIX) and LUCA Campus Lemmens Search options within the Music Catalog

Start your search with a well-chosen keyword and select the appropriate scope: Scores, Audio or Music Catalog. If you know precisely what you are looking for, click on advanced search for a highly targeted search. You can always refine your search results via the filters in the left column.

Within the scope Scores, it is possible to search and refine by instrument and number of players. Enter the name of the instrument as a search term in the search bar or refine your results using the filters Instrumentation and Number of Players. Please note: the search engine only works with English names.
