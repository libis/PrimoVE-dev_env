What can you find in LIMO?
  • Scores: mostly sheet music from all LIBISnet institutions
  • Audio: audio-recordings from all LIBISnet institutions
  • Music catalog: Printed publications concerning music, from LUCA Libraries Campus Lemmens, KU Leuven Libraries Artes LMUZ and Matrix LMAT, and electronic publications from LUCA

Search options
  • Choose the source type (under the search box) if you are looking for something else than sheet music
  • For sheet music: use instruments as search terms and filter on player total, refine your search with classification codes

Examples
  • Search terms: "violin, viola, violoncello" and filter with 4 players: string quartets, sort your results on author to view your results alphabetically on composer surname
  • Search term: "oboe 262" and filter with 2 players: oboe with keyboard accompaniment
