What does the Limo AI Assistant do?

AI is coming to Limo! The Limo AI Assistant allows you to enter your question in natural language. Using GenAI, the tool converts it to a search query that the Limo search engine can understand:

Click the 'Limo AI Assistant' button in the top menu to start using this new search tool:

  • Get a first impression of your search topic thanks to a short AI-generated answer, with inline source citations
  • Explore more sources on your topic via "View more results"
  • Find inspiration for related queries through the "Related research questions" under the sources overview
