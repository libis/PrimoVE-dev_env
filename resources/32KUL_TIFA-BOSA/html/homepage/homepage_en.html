Which collections does Limo put at your disposal?

The library of the Federal Public Service Policy and Support (FPS BOSA) is one of the federal libraries. It is managed by the FPS BOSA's Directorate-General Recruitment and Development.
This library provides quick access to information through publications and multimedia supports. It puts about 13,000 reference works and some hundred periodicals at everyone's disposal in the following fields:

  • Administrative science
  • Social sciences
  • Management and human resources management
  • Project management, quality management, knowledge management, conflict management, professional and personal development
  • Communication
  • Language learning and information technology
  • "Learning and Development", didactic methodologies, coaching, team management,
