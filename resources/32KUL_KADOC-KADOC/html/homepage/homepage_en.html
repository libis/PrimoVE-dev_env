Welcome to the catalog of KADOC-KU Leuven, Documentation and Research Center on Religion, Culture and Society

What can you find in Limo? Archival units and audiovisual documentation (information from inventories) held in KADOC. Search in Limo or browse our archives overview (click on 'Localize in archive plan' in the left hand menu).

Printed and digital publications held:
  • in KADOC (Documentation and Research Centre for Religion, Culture and Society)
  • in the collection of our partner Foundation Bethune (Marke)
  • in other LIBISnet libraries
Authorized information on organizations, individuals and families, events and buildings, periodicals and archives (ODIS)

Both physical, digitized and born-digital materials can be found in Limo.

It is possible to search on collections
  • All collections (print and digital) (held in Leuven and Marke)
  • Collection KADOC (held in KADOC)
  • Collection EVADOC (held in KADOC)
  • Collection de Bethune (held in Marke)
  • Parish Journals / Kerk en Leven (digital collection with full text search)
  • Curated collections, see at the top of the page (digital publications held in KADOC)
