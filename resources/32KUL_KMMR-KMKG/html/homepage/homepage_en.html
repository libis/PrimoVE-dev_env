What can you find in Limo?

RMAH

The research library of the Royal Museums of Art and History specializes in archaeology and arts of the five continents, ranging from prehistory to the present day. The library serves not only to museum staff, but also researchers, students and the general public.

MIM

The scientific library of the Musical Instruments Museum (MIM) is specialised in organology, the history of western music, traditional music and ethnomusicology. The library also hold a number of special collections such as the Robert Pernet fund, dedicated to Belgian jazz music, and the Fund Christian Poché, dedicated to traditional world music.
